Diluc Ragvindr

Biography:

Diluc Ragnvindr is a playable character in Genshin Impact. He is the current owner of the Dawn Winery and a nobleman of high esteem in Mondstadt society. While he appears apathetic about the affairs of the city, he protects it as the "Darknight Hero".

Interests:

  • Eagles
  • Pyrotechnics
  • Claymores
